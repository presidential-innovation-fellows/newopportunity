What is The Opportunity Project?

Launched by the Obama Administration in 2016, The Opportunity Project empowers families, communities, local leaders, and the media to find the information and resources they need to thrive. By curating Federal and local open data sets and facilitating collaboration among communities, technologists, and government, the Opportunity Project aims to accomplish three goals:

Help people use information

Help people use information about neighborhood-level resources.

Make government data accessible

Make government data sets readily accessible, user friendly, and useful.

Facilitate collaboration

Facilitate development collaborations among the tech sector, government, and communities.
