What is The Opportunity Project?

The Opportunity Project is a platform to build more just and equitable communities through open data, technology and collaboration. Together, we are creating tools that empower people with information about the resources we all need to thrive. The Opportunity Project is:

Empowering people with information

New digital tools are helping families, community leaders, and local officials make more informed decisions, access new resources, and expand opportunity for all.

Making government data accessible

A carefully curated combination of Federal and local open data on access to neighborhood-level resources, like proximity to jobs, affordable housing and quality schools is making government data user friendly and easily transformed into meaningful tools that help people solve challenges in their everyday lives.
